The InsightsOut Leadership Circle

Small group coaching for decisions you should not carry alone.

The Leadership Circle is a four week experience for founders, managers, and professionals navigating a difficult decision, changing role, team pressure, or rapid change. Bring one real situation. Leave with clearer direction, practical action, and a trusted peer group.

Join the Interest List See What You Get

Four weeks · Four guided group sessions · 8 to 10 people · Online and San Francisco

Who it is for

For people carrying a real leadership or work challenge.

You do not need a particular title. You need a real situation you are ready to examine honestly and act on.

Founders and leaders

Sort competing priorities, difficult decisions, relationships, and the pressure of carrying responsibility.

Managers and team leads

Lead people through uncertainty, communicate clearly, and respond to resistance without carrying everything alone.

Professionals navigating change

Work through a changing role, a transition, or a question about where your experience creates value next.

What you get

A trusted place to think, act, and return.

Bring one real situation through four weeks of guided coaching. Use the group to see it more clearly, take action in real life, and decide what comes next.

  • Four guided 90 minute group coaching sessions
  • A trusted group of 8 to 10 people
  • One real conversation, decision, or experiment between meetings
  • Accountability and a final 30 day direction
