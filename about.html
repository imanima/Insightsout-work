About InsightsOut

InsightsOut helps people lead through rapid change.

We create places to think clearly, learn with others, and take practical action when work is changing fast.

Why we exist

Change moves fast. People still need time to understand what it means.

New technology, shifting roles, and pressure to adapt can change relationships, decisions, identity, and livelihood.

People need room to process what is happening, find what they can influence, and take part in shaping the future of their work. AI is one important part of that change, but the work begins with people.

What guides us

Human leadership through rapid change.

People need room to process

Emotion is part of change, not a distraction from it.

Human agency is the goal

People need a voice and a real ability to shape what happens next.

Leadership can begin anywhere

Anyone can face reality, make a choice, and act with others.

About the founder

Nima understands both systems and people.

Nima Imani founded InsightsOut after more than a decade working across software engineering, data, AI, solution architecture, consulting, and technology leadership, including EY and Neo4j.

Before technology, he worked as a teacher and tutor. That is where he first saw how much fear and identity can sit underneath a hard problem, and how the right support can restore confidence.

Today, Nima is an ICF ACC coach and trained facilitator. He brings technical depth, coaching presence, and practical experience leading change.
