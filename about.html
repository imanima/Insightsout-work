My background

I have spent my career working on change from both sides.

For more than 10 years, I have worked across software engineering, data, AI, solution architecture, and enterprise transformation—including digital transformation work at EY and customer-facing solution work at Neo4j.

That experience taught me that transformation is never only technical. New tools change how people make decisions, work together, and understand their own value.

My work later expanded into coaching and facilitation. As an ICF-certified and ontologically trained coach, I support founders, technical leaders, and teams navigating uncertainty, identity shifts, leadership pressure, and change.

I founded InsightsOut to bring those two bodies of work together: deep experience with technology and close attention to how people move through change.
