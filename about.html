About InsightsOut

Human leadership for work that is changing fast.

InsightsOut helps people and organizations make sense of rapid change, build practical capability, and shape new ways of working they can understand and own.

Meet Nima See How We Help

Nima Imani, founder

Nima has spent his career helping people make difficult change real.

Nima has more than a decade of experience leading digital transformation and technology change across software engineering, data, AI, solution architecture, consulting, and technology leadership, including work with EY and Neo4j.

He has seen that transformation does not succeed through strategy and tools alone. It becomes real when people understand what is changing, can talk honestly about the pressure, and have a meaningful role in shaping how the work moves forward.

Alongside his technology career, Nima trained as a leadership coach and facilitator. As an ICF ACC coach, he works with founders, managers, and professionals facing difficult decisions, changing roles, team pressure, and new levels of responsibility.

He founded InsightsOut to bring these two bodies of work together: practical experience leading transformation and deep attention to people, learning, and leadership.

Why InsightsOut exists

Transformation only works when people can make sense of it.

New technology can change roles, relationships, decisions, and expectations faster than people can absorb. When the human questions are ignored, adoption becomes fragile and leaders carry more pressure.

InsightsOut creates the space, structure, and practical learning people need to understand the change, exercise judgment, and take part in shaping what happens next.
