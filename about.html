About InsightsOut

Change affects people before it changes an organization chart.

InsightsOut helps people understand what is changing, recover a sense of agency, and move forward with other people.

Why it exists

New tools and plans are rarely enough.

When work changes quickly, people worry about their value, identity, relationships, and future. Leaders feel pressure to move faster while their teams need honesty, steadiness, and a voice in what comes next.

InsightsOut creates practical spaces where people can name what is happening, work through real choices, learn together, and take action.

About Nima

A teacher, engineer, technology leader, founder, and coach.

Nima Imani began as a teacher at a UN sponsored school in Turkey. After arriving in the United States as a refugee, he learned English, studied computational mathematics at the University of Washington, and built a career across engineering, data, consulting, and technology leadership.

He founded InsightsOut to combine his experience with complex systems and his commitment to the people living inside them.
