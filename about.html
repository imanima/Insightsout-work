About InsightsOut

InsightsOut is the human side of AI change.

We help people and organizations process what is changing, protect human agency, and make clear choices about what comes next.

Why we exist

Technology moves fast. People still need time to understand what it means.

AI changes more than tools. It can change roles, relationships, decisions, identity, and livelihood.

People need room to process the change, decide what should stay human, and take part in shaping the future of their work.

What guides us

Human leadership for the age of AI.

People need room to process

Emotion is part of change, not a distraction from it.

Human agency is the goal

People need a voice and a real ability to shape what happens next.

Leadership can begin anywhere

Anyone can face reality, make a choice, and act with others.

How it shows up

Workshops, cohorts, organizational labs, and field notes.

InsightsOut was founded in San Francisco by Nima Imani after more than a decade working across engineering, consulting, technology leadership, and change.
