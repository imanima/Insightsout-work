About

Change isn't a theory here. It's a biography.

InsightsOut was founded by Nima Imani — teacher, refugee, mathematician, technologist, consultant, coach. Every chapter taught a different piece of what it takes to move through disruption. This work is all of them, together.

Teacher

It started with a love of learning and helping people grow — the thread that never broke.

Refugee

A lived understanding of disruption, reinvention, and resilience. Change was never abstract.

Technologist

Mathematics, engineering, and a decade in technical roles — tech lead and solution architect at EY, senior solution engineer at Neo4j. Real credibility in complex systems.

Consultant

Years inside digital transformation, watching the same truth repeat: adoption never fails on the technology. It fails on the humans nobody prepared.

Coach

ICF-certified, trained in ontological coaching — language, emotions, body, beliefs, ways of being. Today Nima coaches founders and technical leaders privately — book time with him here.

Now

In San Francisco, at the center of the AI shift, helping people become capable, ethical, creative leaders in the next transformation.

The philosophy is simple: human at the center, emotion as data, ethics as practice, leadership through change.
