InsightsOut research

What is AI change doing to people and work?

We collect what people are experiencing as roles change, AI enters everyday work, and leaders try to help teams adapt. We look for patterns that can make the future of work more human, practical, and grounded in real experience.

How we learn

Real people, real work, and change as it happens.

Our learning comes from community events, workshops, organizational programs, and conversations with people living through the transition.

What we follow

  • How AI is changing roles, identity, and working life
  • What helps AI learning become useful everyday practice
  • How managers and teams lead through uncertainty
  • What people need to shape the future of their work

We share only what the evidence supports and protect participant privacy.
