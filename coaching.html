Questions

Straight answers.

Is this coaching or consulting?

This is coaching. I bring founder and technology context, but the work is not about giving you generic advice. It is about helping you see more clearly, notice your patterns, and lead from a more grounded place.

Is this therapy?

No. Coaching is not therapy and does not diagnose or treat mental health conditions. We may work with emotions, stress, and overwhelm, but always in the context of leadership, building, and personal growth.

Is this only for founders?

No. I work with founders, executives, managers, and leaders whose roles are changing — anyone carrying real stakes: a team, customers, investors, or a major transition.

What's the difference between 1:1 and The Inner Board?

1:1 is private, tailored, and moves at your pace. The Inner Board works on shared ground — decisions, overwhelm, relationships — with the honesty and perspective of peers who carry similar weight. Many leaders do both.

What happens on a discovery call?

We talk about what you are building, what you are navigating, and what kind of support would be useful. If there is a fit, I'll share what working together could look like.
