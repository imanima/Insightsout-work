Questions

Straight answers before we talk.

Who do you work with?

I work with founders, managers, executives, and people stepping into a larger or changing role. Most are carrying meaningful responsibility and navigating uncertainty.

Is this coaching or consulting?

This is coaching. I bring business and technology context, but I do not hand you a generic answer. I help you see clearly, challenge assumptions, and make choices you can own.

Do I need a perfectly defined goal?

No. Many clients begin because something feels unclear, heavy, or stuck. Understanding what needs attention is part of the work.

What happens in the first conversation?

We talk about what is happening, what you are carrying, and what kind of support may help. We both decide whether working together feels right.
