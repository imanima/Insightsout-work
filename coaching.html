One-on-one coaching

Coaching for people leading through change.

Coaching with Nima is for the person underneath the role — navigating change, leadership, identity, creativity, and agency in a rapidly shifting world. We work at the level of emotion, perspective, action, and possibility, so you can lead from a more grounded and powerful place.

Book a Coaching Call Learn About Nima

Coaching may be for you if…

  • Your role is changing faster than your sense of who you are in it.
  • You're leading others through uncertainty while carrying your own.
  • You want to adopt AI without abandoning the values that make your work yours.
  • You're successful on paper and quietly exhausted in practice.
  • You're ready to look at what's underneath — not just optimize the surface.

How it works

Simple format, deep work.

One-on-one sessions
