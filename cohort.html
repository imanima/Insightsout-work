Find Your Next Move

Your role is changing. Work out what comes next with a small group.

A four-week guided cohort for people whose work, role, or sense of value is changing faster than they can comfortably process.

Join the Interest List

Four weeks · 8 to 10 people · Separate online and San Francisco groups

The four sessions

Clarity first. Then a move you can actually make.

Each week builds on the last, while leaving room for the real situations people bring.

Name what is changing

Make the pressure, fear, and uncertainty specific enough to work with.

See what you still bring

Identify the judgment, relationships, experience, and strengths that remain valuable.

Choose your direction

Decide what to learn, what to stop, and where to place your attention next.

Commit to a real move

Leave with a grounded plan and people who will ask what happened.

Between sessions

The work continues in real life.

You return with what you tried, what you learned, and where you got stuck. The group helps you stay accountable without turning the experience into homework for its own sake.

  • A short reflection after each session
  • One conversation, experiment, or learning goal
  • A check-in with another participant
  • A final direction and 30-day commitment
