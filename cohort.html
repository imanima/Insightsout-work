The details

Know what you are joining.

How does the cohort run?

Four weekly 90 minute live sessions. Groups are offered separately online and in San Francisco. The cohort is limited to 8 to 10 people.

How much time is needed between sessions?

Plan for about one hour each week. The work should fit into something you already need to do, not create another burden.

Do I need a technical background?

No. People from any field are welcome. AI may be part of the change, but the cohort is about your real work, choices, relationships, and direction.

Can my employer pay?

Yes. We can provide a simple description and invoice for professional development support.

When are dates and price confirmed?

Everyone on the interest list receives the dates, location, full commitment, and price before deciding whether to join. Applying does not commit you.
