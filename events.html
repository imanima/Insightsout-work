Four recurring events

Clear questions. Practical labs. Real connection.

The first two run monthly. The other two return throughout the year as the community grows.

MonthlyRole change

My Role Is Changing. What Do I Do Now?

For people between jobs or worried that their role is losing value. Map what is changing, what you still bring, and what direction is worth testing next.

You leave with a role map and one next move.
MonthlyManagers

My Team Is Overwhelmed. How Do I Lead?

For managers trying to communicate clearly, respond to emotion, and help people move through uncertainty without pretending to have every answer.

You leave with a conversation plan and one action for your team.
SeasonalFounders and leaders

Everyone Looks to Me. Who Can I Talk To?

For people carrying decisions, responsibility, and loneliness. Bring the pressure you cannot easily discuss at work.

You leave with perspective, support, and a decision you can own.
SeasonalHuman judgment

What Should Stay Human?

Work through a real decision about what to automate, where people must remain responsible, and how to protect trust while work changes.

You leave with clear boundaries and a practical way forward.

Every event is a working lab

Bring something real

Work on it together

Use guided reflection, practical tools, and thoughtful peer conversation.

Choose what comes next

Leave with one action, conversation, or experiment that belongs to you.

Research from the room

The events also help us understand change as people live through it.

Optional anonymous reflections help us identify patterns around role change, overwhelm, agency, trust, and responsible adoption. We publish only aggregated findings.
