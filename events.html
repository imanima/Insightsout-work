Workshop series

Five practical workshops. Start with what feels most real.

Each 90 minute session starts with a real situation and ends with one practical move. New San Francisco and online dates will appear on Luma.

Managers and team leads90 minutes

My Team Is Overwhelmed. How Do I Lead?

For managers carrying pressure from above while their teams absorb new priorities, tools, and expectations. Understand what is driving the overwhelm and decide what your team needs most now.

Leave with a clearer read and a two week leadership plan. Get the next date
Founders and startup leaders90 minutes

From AI Overwhelm to Focus

For founders facing too many tools, too many decisions, and too little time to think. Separate what matters now from what can wait.

Leave with one clear priority and one AI bet worth making. Get the next date
Open to everyone90 minutes

What Should Stay Human?

For anyone deciding what to give to AI and what should remain human. Bring one real decision and examine the benefits, possible harms, responsibilities, and values with other people.

Leave with a completed decision map and a boundary you can explain. Get the next date
Roles and transitions90 minutes

My Role Is Changing. What’s My Next Move?

For people whose work is shifting, people between jobs, and anyone quietly wondering what they still bring. Separate what is changing from the worst case story and find a direction to test.

Leave with one pathfinding step that creates evidence, not more anxiety. Get the next date
Consultants and small business ownersHands on lab

I Don’t Know Where to Start With AI. Start Here.

For people who want a safe, practical place to begin. Bring one task that takes too much of your week and build a useful first workflow in plain English.

Leave with one workflow running and a place to keep learning. Get the next date
